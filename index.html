    Incubator Coaching is a non profit corporation providing coaching and mentoring services to young adults and others in underserved communities.

The Mission

Incubator Coaching goal is to provide coaching and mentoring services to youth and adults who otherwise would not be exposed to the benefits of having such support. By providing coaching to a broad and diverse community, Incubator Coaching will offer opportunities that are currently only available to folks who are more highly educated or who are socio economically privileged. Incubator Coaching will level the playing field for people who can enter or advance in an education system and work force geared towards those with more opportunities.

How It Works

Incubator Coaching will work on an unique model of offering coaching hours to our clients through a grant making process while also offering the opportunity for pro bono coaching hours to coaches seeking certification or other coaches who have already been certified and are looking to increase their pro bono hours.
Organizations can apply for mentorship or coaching hours though our grant process. They will then be matched to appropriate coaches by region, field of expertise or experience.

Status
